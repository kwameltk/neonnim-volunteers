Introduction

Embark on a transformative journey with Nea Onnim Volunteers , dedicated to making a lasting impact in the heart of Kwahu, Ghana since 2019. Join us in collaborating with six vibrant communities across diverse sectors, where your involvement promises not only a life-changing experience but contributes significantly to our mission of shaping a brighter future for the youth. At Nea Onnim, we advocate for the fundamental rights of education, sustainable mental health care, and nurturing the aspirations of children, especially girls, guiding them towards a promising tomorrow.

Together, let's magnify our influence and collectively unlock the power of knowledge, believing that by sharing our wisdom, we can inspire each other to reach new heights and manifest our dreams. Join us on this extraordinary journey where your contribution becomes a catalyst for positive change.

    What is Included

    Utility Bills
  • We will take care of covering your utilities in the accommodation, encompassing water, electricity, and gas expenses.
    • Welcome Tour & Orientation
  • Upon your arrival, enjoy a welcoming breakfast to recharge from your journey. Following that, we'll guide you around the local area for any shopping needs and familiarize you with key local points of interest.
    • Accomodation
  • Accommodation is arranged in a communal Volunteer Residence , where you will be furnished with either a single or bunk bed, along with bedding. for washing of things, there is a laundry service and you can also learn to wash things the native way.
    • Dynamic Staff
  • Our team of local Ghanaians will be available at your volunteer residence and placement site to assist with any questions or provide guidance as needed.
    • Organisation Administrative Costs
  • Nea Onnim Volunteers utilizes the fees charged to sustain administrative costs, ensuring that the organization can efficiently operate and provide a seamless volunteering experience. Your contribution directly supports the logistics, coordination, and support services that make our volunteer programs impactful and well-organized.
    • Humanitarian Support
  • The fees paid by volunteers play a crucial role in supporting community development initiatives, fostering a humanitarian impact. These funds contribute to projects that address pressing needs within the community, such as education, healthcare, and infrastructure improvements. By allocating a portion of the fees to these initiatives, volunteers actively participate in creating positive, sustainable change in the communities they serve.

    • What's not Excluded?

    Placement Location Transport
  • Volunteer guides will accompany you to a nearby project site from the accommodation. Should you desire to explore more remote rural communities or visit specific project sites for placement activities, you are responsible for covering the transportation cost in a shared taxi, which is approximately $1 from the volunteer house or homestay to the placement sites. After three guided visits, you'll gain familiarity and be able to navigate independently.
    • Visa & Travel Vaccinations
  • While we are readily available to provide guidance and assistance with visa applications, it's important to note that you are responsible for covering the associated costs, including travel vaccinations such as the mandatory yellow fever injection and certificate.
    • Food & Grocery Shopping
  • As this is a self-catering placement, you'll have access to the kitchen in the houses for preparing your meals or the option to purchase from numerous street vendors in town. Additionally, a refrigerator in the volunteer room allows you to store perishables like milk and vegetables, granting convenient access to the adjoining kitchen at any time.
    • Flights & Travel
  • You are responsible for your flight and travel expenses. Upon arrival at Accra – Kotoka International Airport, our team member, Bright, will be there to welcome you. From the airport, you will proceed to the bus station or have the option to join a pre-arranged car, the cost of which is detailed in the expense table.
    • All fees are expected to be paid fully in before volunteers arrive in ghana for smooth operations and administration by our local director . Volunteer Accomodation

At Nea Onnim Volunteers, we offer two distinct accommodation options to suit the preferences of our volunteers. The first option involves staying with a local host family, where volunteers have the unique opportunity to immerse themselves in Ghanaian culture and hospitality, under the guidance of our founder, Kwame. You can read more on volunteer house Volunteer House

Second option fosters a close-knit community atmosphere with nature environmentally friendly space Obomeng Kwahu. Alternatively, volunteers may choose to stay in our dedicated volunteer house, offering a more independent and private living environment. Both accommodation options feature personal kitchens, allowing volunteers to prepare meals at their convenience, as part of our self-catering program. Whichever option you choose, rest assured that your comfort and needs are our top priority at Nea Onnim Volunteers.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you need more answers to possible questions, you'll find them below.

Will I be provided with food and meals?

^

No, Food is excluded from the fees paid but there is a kitchen where you can always cook your food. Local food vendors are easily accessible for volunteers to purchase additional meals and snacks.

Can I easily find food to buy?

^

Local food vendors are easily accessible for volunteers to purchase additional meals and snacks.

Can I drink tap water?

^

It's recommended to drink Sachet, bottled or filtered water to avoid any potential health issues. If you chose host family stay, Sachet water is free at your disposal.

Are there other shops available to replenish my supplies?

^

There are local shops nearby where volunteers can replenish their supplies as needed. There is also a big supermarket in nkawkaw which you can make all your basic needs.

I have data roaming - can I use this?

^

Yes, you can use data roaming for internet access.

I have an unlocked phone - will I be able to use this?

^

An unlocked phone can be used with a local SIM card for communication.

How can I get online and stay in touch

^

Volunteers can get online using Wi-Fi available at the volunteer house at a fee depending on the volume of data been used. Most important, every sim card is enabled to access internet.

How will I get to the volunteer house from the airport?

^

A staff member will pick you up from the airport and transport you to the volunteer house. There is a bus transport same day that heads to Kwahu but you can also hire a pickup saloon car.

Which airport do I need to fly into?

^

You need to fly into Kotoka International Airport in Accra.

What season is best to volunteer in Kwahu, Ghana?

^

Each season in Kwahu, Ghana, offers a unique and enriching experience for volunteers. In Ghana, there are two main seasons: the rainy season and the dry season. The rainy season typically occurs from April to October, characterized by lush green landscapes and occasional rainfall. Despite the rain, Kwahu is particularly serene during this time, providing a peaceful atmosphere for volunteering activities. On the other hand, the dry season spans from November to March, marked by sunny weather and cooler temperatures, making it ideal for outdoor activities and exploration. Ultimately, the best season to volunteer depends on your preferences and the type of experience you seek.

Volunteer Free Time

Hiking and Nature Exploration: Explore the breathtaking landscapes of Kwahu through scenic hikes and nature walks. Discover hidden trails, majestic waterfalls, and lush forests while immersing yourself in the natural beauty of the region.
Cultural Immersion: Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Kwahu by visiting local villages, attending traditional festivals, and participating in cultural ceremonies. Engage with community members, learn about their customs and traditions, and gain a deeper appreciation for Ghanaian culture.
Outdoor Adventure: Embark on thrilling outdoor adventures such as zip-lining, rock climbing, and canopy tours. Experience the adrenaline rush of exploring Kwahu's rugged terrain and stunning landscapes while challenging yourself to new adventures.
Arts and Crafts Workshops: Tap into your creative side by participating in arts and crafts workshops led by local artisans. Learn traditional Ghanaian crafts such as bead-making, pottery, and batik fabric dyeing while creating unique souvenirs to take home.

Exploration: Indulge your taste buds in the vibrant flavors of Ghanaian cuisine by sampling local dishes at street markets, food stalls, and traditional eateries. Take a cooking class to learn how to prepare authentic Ghanaian dishes and impress your friends and family back home.
Community Service Projects Give back to the local community by volunteering for community service projects such as tree planting, clean-up campaigns, and educational outreach programs. Make a positive impact while fostering meaningful connections with community members.
Sports and Recreation: Stay active and enjoy recreational activities such as soccer, basketball, and volleyball with local youth and community members. Join pickup games or organize friendly tournaments to bond with locals and promote healthy living.

Cultural Performances: Immerse yourself in the vibrant rhythms and dances of Ghana by attending cultural performances and live music concerts. Experience traditional drumming, dance performances, and storytelling sessions that showcase the rich cultural heritage of the region.
Shopping and Souvenir Hunting: Explore local markets and craft stalls to shop for unique souvenirs and handcrafted goods. Browse through colorful textiles, intricate beadwork, and artisanal crafts to find the perfect memento of your time in Kwahu.
Relaxation and Leisure: Take time to unwind and relax amidst the serene surroundings of Kwahu. Lounge by the pool, enjoy a leisurely stroll, or simply soak in the breathtaking views of the countryside. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Kwahu offers something for everyone to enjoy during their free time.
