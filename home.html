When Can I Join


At Nea Onnim Volunteers, we value the meaningful impact that our volunteers make, which is why we have a minimum stay requirement to ensure maximum benefits. For education projects, we ask for a commitment of at least eight(8) weeks. However, if your schedule allows for a shorter duration, we offer diverse projects like Renovation, Painting,Girls in Entrepreneurship,Mentorship, Sports, and Sanitation Awareness Program, starting from just one week, providing flexibility to contribute according to your availability READ MORE....

Why Choose Us

We Promise you a Memorable Volunteer Experience in Ghana as a duly registered Organization.Join us on a transcendent volunteering journey with Nea Onnim Volunteers, where each moment becomes a catalyst for profound change. Unlike conventional programs, we redefine volunteering by tailoring experiences to your unique skills and passions, ensuring a direct and meaningful impact. Immerse yourself in the heart of Kwahu, Ghana, where our vibrant local staffs and a welcoming volunteer community create a conducive environment for impactful contributions in our Volunteer Placements.

Our History

Nea Onnim Volunteers has been deeply entrenched in the vibrant tapestry of Kwahu communities, notably in Obomeng, Atibie, Mpraeso, and Besease. Since our inception in 2016, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to fostering sustainable community development initiatives.
