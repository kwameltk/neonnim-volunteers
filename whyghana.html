Explore Heritage

CENTRE OF HERITAGE

Immerse yourself in Ghana's rich history and heritage, Explore Ghana's rich history and vibrant culture through its iconic landmarks. From ancient structures dating back to 1500 BC to the profound impact of the transatlantic slave trade, Ghana offers a captivating journey for history enthusiasts. Discover well-preserved castles and forts along the coast, remnants of a tumultuous era marked by European traders. Experience poignant history firsthand at landmarks like Elmina Castle in the Central Region, where echoes of the past bring centuries of stories to life. Head to Accra to visit the National Museum, offering insight into colonial times, and the Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum, a testament to Ghana's progress since independence. Explore colonial neighborhoods and Jamestown's fishing community for a taste of local life. Each landmark provides a glimpse into Ghana's rich heritage, essential for any adventure in this culturally diverse nation.
